# United States Capitol Police (united-states-capitol-police)
The United States Capitol Police is a federal law enforcement agency responsible for protecting the U.S. Capitol Building, members of Congress, and visitors to the Capitol complex. The agency is tasked with maintaining security and order within the Capitol grounds, conducting background checks on individuals entering the buildings, and responding to emergencies and threats. Capitol Police officers also provide security for large events and demonstrations taking place on Capitol Hill. Overall, the United States Capitol Police plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and security of the legislative branch of the U.S. government.
